Max Temperature

Visualizations

This figure shows the relation between latitude and maximum temperature in Farenheit. The weather is warmer as location approaches the equator. This time of year the southern hemisphere is warmer than the northern hemisphere, which should be kept in mind when analyzing this data.

The maximum temperature is centralized around the equator, latitude 0. This makes sense since the warmest part of Earth tends to be near the equator.

Latitude increases as the maximum temperature decreases. As previously mentioned, this makes sense due to the warmest part of Earth being centralized around the equator, so as you move away from the equator, temperature tends to drop.
